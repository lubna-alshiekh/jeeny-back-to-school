KSA Drivers Campaign

A two-week interactive in-app experience designed to increase driver ride volume through daily missions, progress, instant wins, and a final graduation reward.

How the experience works

1. Daily targetEach driver sees a target based on their cohort.
2. Pack unlockReaching the target unlocks one three-card sticker pack.
3. Album collectionDuplicates are allowed, while missing-card protection improves fairness near the end.
4. Draw entryDrivers who complete the full nine-card album enter the final draw.
Use the cohort buttons, simulate rides, open the pack, and switch to the Album tab to demonstrate the complete flow.
